about

i don’t really think of this as just working with data. most of the time i’m trying to make sense of systems: technical, human, and the invisible ones in between.

my work usually sits somewhere between:

  • data modeling
  • distributed systems
  • decision-making
  • truth under uncertainty

a few things i keep coming back to:

  • good data models say something about how you believe reality works
  • simplicity usually takes more effort than complexity
  • most systems fail because nobody fully understands them
